# Smart Scale – AI-Powered Smart Kitchen Scale

Create a complete **high-fidelity, clickable Figma prototype** for **Smart Scale**, a premium AI-powered smart kitchen scale with an integrated **4.3-inch touchscreen**.

Smart Scale combines precise weight measurement with an integrated camera and AI-powered food recognition. When a user places food on the scale, the camera automatically scans the food, identifies it, measures its weight, calculates calories, macronutrients, and micronutrients, and asks whether the user wants to track the food in their connected mobile app.

The objective is to create a premium user experience that feels effortless, intelligent, modern, and enjoyable—comparable to Apple's design philosophy without copying Apple's UI.

---

# Product Vision

Smart Scale should transform food tracking into a seamless experience.

The interaction should require as few steps as possible while making the user feel confident that the AI is accurate and trustworthy.

The experience should feel:

* Friendly

The interface should be optimized for a **4.3-inch touchscreen** with large touch targets, excellent readability, intuitive navigation, and minimal cognitive load.

Create a **fully clickable prototype** with realistic interactions, animations, and screen transitions.
